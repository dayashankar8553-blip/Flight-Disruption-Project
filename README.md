# ✈️ Flight Disruption Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes flight disruptions and their financial, operational, and geopolitical impact using Power BI. It provides insights into delays, cancellations, diversions, and their effect on airline performance and revenue.

The dashboard combines data storytelling and interactive visuals to highlight key challenges faced by the aviation industry.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze flight disruptions across airlines and time.
- Understand the financial impact of delays and cancellations.
- Evaluate airline efficiency and performance.
- Identify key drivers of revenue loss.
- Study the impact of geopolitical events and NOTAM alerts.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Overview
- Total Flights: **500**
- Disrupted Flights: **500**
- Disruption Rate: **100%**
- Avg Delay: **65 minutes**

📈 Key Visuals:
- Flight Disruption vs Revenue Loss Trend.
- Disruption by Airline.
- Distribution of Disruption Types.

📌 Insight:
All flights experienced disruptions, indicating a **system-wide operational issue** rather than isolated incidents.

---

### 2️⃣ Analysis
📊 Key Findings:
- Disruptions peak around **September**
- Airlines show variability in performance
- Equal contribution from:
  - Cancellation (25%)
  - Delay (25%)
  - Diversion (25%)
  - No Disruption (25%)

✈️ Airline Insights:
- Emirates and IndiGo show higher disruption levels.
- Performance varies significantly across months.

---

### 3️⃣ Financial Impact
- Estimated Loss: **~$1 Billion**
- Avg Ticket Price: **12.64K**
- Cost per Passenger: **12.56K**

📉 Insights:
- Cancellations contribute highest losses (~$310M).
- Delays & diversions also contribute significantly.
- Increase in affected passengers leads to higher revenue loss.

📌 Key Driver:
Passenger volume is a major factor influencing financial loss.

---

### 4️⃣ Geopolitical Impact
- Fuel Cost Increase: **474K**
- NOTAM Issued: **500**

🌍 Insights:
- NOTAM-related disruptions contribute nearly **50% of total losses**
- Conflict zones (Iran, Israel, Gulf, Lebanon) increase operational cost.
- External factors significantly affect airline performance.

---

### 5️⃣ Key Insights & Recommendations

### 🔍 Key Insights:
- 100% disruption rate highlights critical operational inefficiencies.
- Strong correlation between disruptions and financial loss.
- Airlines with lower efficiency (~60–65%) incur higher losses (~$1M+).
- High dependency on external factors like geopolitics and regulations.

### ✅ Recommendations:
- Improve operational efficiency to reduce delays and cancellations.
- Optimize passenger handling systems.
- Monitor geopolitical risks and NOTAM alerts proactively.
- Implement predictive analytics for disruption management.
